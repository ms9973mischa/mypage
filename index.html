All About Netball

Netball is a ball sport played on a rectangular court by two teams of seven players. The objective is to shoot a ball through the defender's goal ring. According to World Netball, the sport is played by more than 20 million people in more than 80 countries.

Netball Compared with Basketball

A common misunderstanding of netball's origins has resulted in the mistaken belief that netball was created to prevent women from playing basketball.

Netball Rules
